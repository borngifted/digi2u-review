Course Overview

Film & Television
Production.

This program covers all aspects of film and television production, ensuring students develop both creative storytelling techniques and technical expertise in a professional setting.

What You'll Learn

Six modules.
End-to-end production.

You'll gain hands-on experience in cinematography, directing, screenwriting, editing, and live broadcasting — using professional cameras, industry-standard software, and the techniques that drive working productions today.

01

Cinematography & Camera Operation

Master the fundamentals of camera angles, framing, lighting, and movement to capture compelling visuals.

02

Directing for Film & TV

Learn how to manage a production, direct actors, and bring your creative vision to life on screen.

03

Screenwriting & Storytelling

Develop the skills to write, structure, and refine scripts for films, TV shows, and digital media.

04

Video Editing & Post-Production

Gain hands-on experience with industry-standard editing software such as Adobe Premiere Pro, DaVinci Resolve, and Final Cut Pro.

05

Live Broadcast & Studio Production

Train in multi-camera setups, green screen technology, and live streaming for television and online content.

06

Sound & Audio Production

Learn sound design, audio mixing, and dialogue recording techniques to enhance video quality.

Course Description

What's included.

Real-world filmmaking and broadcasting experience

Our Film & Television Production Course is designed to provide students with real-world filmmaking and broadcasting experience. The program includes:

  • Hands-on training in cinematography, directing, and editing
  • Live production experience with studio and on-location shoots
  • Technical training with professional-grade cameras, lighting, and audio equipment
  • Story development and screenwriting workshops
  • Collaboration with industry professionals to gain real-world insights

By the end of this program, students will have produced their own short films, television segments, and media projects — gaining the confidence and technical expertise needed to thrive in the film and TV industry.
