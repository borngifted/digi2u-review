C. Cherry Foundation

Foundation Partner · Newark, NJ

A 501(c)(3) supporting Newark communities

The C. Cherry Foundation is a 501(c)(3) tax-exempt nonprofit organization dedicated to providing vital services to communities in Newark, New Jersey. Their mission focuses on supporting underserved populations through essential resources and community-driven initiatives.

Program Collaboration: Digi2U proudly partnered with the C. Cherry Foundation in support of their community events and provided hands-on production training for students.

Digi2U partnered with Art 4 Change to create The Spin-Off Open Deck — a hands-on production training program paired with a live music showcase, providing students with real-world event production experience.

45-Week Program
Key Initiative · The Spin-Off Open Deck

Live Production Training Class

A 45-week program featuring a fully produced DJ music showcase. Students gained practical skills across every aspect of live event production.

Skills Developed

  • Sound Mixing and Recording
  • Multi-camera video recording (4 cameras)
  • Streaming and live video switching
  • Theatrical and spot lighting
  • Stage rigging (setup and breakdown)
  • Safety and blocking

Production Specs

  • 2,000 sq. ft. venue setup
  • 8×16 stage with state-of-the-art equipment
  • Bose F1 L1 Series Sound System
  • Multiple Shure wireless Microphone Systems
  • Yamaha mixing main console
  • Sling Studio & Blackmagic Streaming units
  • Sony HD video cameras
  • Interactive video and graphic display wall
  • Theatrical lighting and smoke machines
  • DJs used Serato DJ software and the Pioneer SRT1000 DJ Control Unit

Students handled setup, operation, and breakdown for each event, applying classroom knowledge in a professional, real-world setting, all under instructor supervision.
