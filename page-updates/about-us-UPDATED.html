Mission & Vision

Empowering communities
through innovation.

Digi2U, a nonprofit 501(c)(3) tax-exempt organization, prepares people for well-paid technology jobs and in-demand businesses through innovative training in STEAM — Science, Technology, Engineering, Arts, and Math.

Our Mission

To equip our communities with the tools, training, and opportunities to succeed.

Our Vision

A world where all individuals, regardless of background, have access to the resources needed to thrive in a technology-driven society.

Our Master Plan

A network of resources
built with our communities.

Develop, provide, and host a network of resources through our website, community campus network, and collaborations — in northeast New Jersey, southwest Atlanta, and beyond.

Our innovative learning opportunities with business and media production enrichment services empower members to facilitate collaborative workflows through shared access to the tools of business and content production — encouraging giving back through mentorship, volunteerism, apprenticeship, and sponsorship.

Our Story

Four locations.
One mission.

The Digi2U model is easily expandable, replicable, and tailored to local and specific needs. We have successfully reached thousands through our community initiatives and have created curriculum, invested in tools and space, and trained over 25 adults and youth in year-long intensives — resulting in graduates with business and career advancement, and youth now in university.

Our collaboration model operates in five cities and works through our advanced virtual environment. Programs are designed for our communities and have been tested through our successful initiatives serving thousands through community work and dozens through media, additive manufacturing, and woodworking training intensives.

With a collaborative spirit, and your support, our approach to programming can become self-sustainable — replicated, expanded, and tailored to our communities' objectives.
